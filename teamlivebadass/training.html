Spartan Workout for 3-21-15


Stretching

  • Workouts always begin with 10 minutes of various stretches.

Warm-up

  • Walking Lunges
  • Bear Crawl
  • Inch Worms
  • Broad Jump
  • Lateral Apes
  • Crab Walks
  • High Knees
  • Jump Rope for 2 minutes

Workout

  • 10 Strict Push-ups
  • 10 Air Squats
  • 10 Box Jumps (24 inches)
  • 5 Over-the-shoulder Throws (30 lb sandbag)
  • 5 Tire Flips
  • Females 2 Tire Flips/25 lb Log Carry for 50 yards
  • 10 Dumbbell Cleans (5 each arm)

Cashout

  • 100 yard sprints x 4
