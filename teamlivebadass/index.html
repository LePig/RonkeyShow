Training and Fun Stuff

Team Live Bad Ass was formed to training for a Spartan Super, a 7-10 mile obstacle course race. We now meet every Sunday morning to train for what's next.

What is Team Live Bad Ass?

Our Mission

We aren't professional athletes. We're just normal, everyday knuckleheads with a goal of living happy and healthier lives. If we can inspire family and friends along the way........ Bad Ass!

Our Workouts

Each Sunday morning we meet and workout as a team. Originally, our workout were very basic...running and lifting. Eventually, workouts began to evolve to hybrid, crossfit inspired workout. Farmer walks, kettlebells, box jumps, etc. All this, with the intention to make us all around athletes.
