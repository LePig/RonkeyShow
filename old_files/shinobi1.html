UFN50

Former StrikeForce champions Ronaldo "Jacare" Souza and Gegard Mousasi rematch in the main event. In their first fight, in Japan's DREAM in 2008, Mousasi won via upkick. Jacare has gone 11-1 with one NC since then, including four straight wins in the UFC. Mousasi has gone 12-2-1 in the same period, most recently dropping a unanimous decision to Lyoto Machida, and then crushing Mark Munoz in the first.

Former Strikeforce heavyweight champion Alistair Overeem fights Ben Rothwell in the co-main. Though the massive Dutchman impressed in his debut, he has since disappointed. Looking to get his name back into title contention, the "Demolition Man" will need to avoid mental errors AND look like the killer we thought he once was. Rothwell has managed to alternate wins and losses thus far in his Octagon. He'll look to break that streak on Friday.

Pettis vs Melendez

Gilbert Melendez laughs off the fact that his next opponent, UFC lightweight champion Anthony Pettis, is going to be featured on a Wheaties box and he won't. "I shop at Whole Foods anyway," Melendez quipped to reporters Thursday.But Melendez stopped joking around at the The Ultimate Fighter 20 media day when it came to the heat between Pettis and Melendez's Skrap Pack running mate, Nate Diaz.

In the leadup to TUF's 20th season, Pettis has seemed more concerned with Diaz than Melendez, his opposing coach on the show and title challenger on Dec. 6. "I definitely want to kick his ass," Pettis said last week of Diaz, who was Melendez's TUF 20 assistant coach. "I was on the show with him every day, I was there seven weeks and he didn't say anything."
